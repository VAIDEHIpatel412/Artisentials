Basic Tips for Using Drawing Tools

  • Start Light: Begin with lighter strokes. You can darken later.
  • Choose the Right Paper: Use 160 GSM or above to prevent smudging and tearing.
  • Keep Tools Sharp: A fine point improves detail and control.
  • Use a Blending Stump: For smooth shading, use blending stumps or tissues.
  • Fix Your Work: Spray fixative to avoid smudging finished charcoal drawings.
